Working with us

A three step process of working with ElectroDaves is as follows.

  • A phone consultation or in person meting is arranged, where we will discuss your projects requirements and get an overview. This is a free consultation, gives us an idea if your project is within our capabilities, the complexity, and involvement of the project. This roughs out the details, and gives you a “ballpark” estimate of what is involved.
  • We will arrange a paid feasibility study or project proposal. This will include the estimate engineering costs, and finalize details such as what components will be used, challenges and solutions in order to achieve your project goals. This sets the project scope and requirements.
  • Once all aspects of your project and costs have been agreed upon, ElectroDave’s will begin designing and manufacturing your project, to meet the project requirements and scope outlined in the proposal.
